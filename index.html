The honest reality checkWhat the data won't tell you
Speech therapy context
The förskola is part of the therapy. Research on multilingual kids with speech/language needs in Sweden is clear: the environment Ivaan is in for 8 hours a day matters as much as the 45-minute session with the therapist. Dropping him into a 151-child Swedish-only kommunal environment cold is not neutral — it can cause regression before progress, especially if his Swedish is the most recent of his four languages.
What a specialpedagog would actually ask
Not "which school has the best grades." They'd ask: What's the adult-to-child ratio in the specific avdelning he'd be in? Is there a specialpedagog on site or shared across buildings? Do staff have experience with children switching between four active languages — not just immigrant kids, but genuinely multilingual kids? None of this is in the public data. All of it matters more than the meritvärde.
The Olympica wildcard
34 children. Montessori. No rush. If what helps Ivaan most is calm and patience, Olympicaförskolan in Täby Centrum may be the best therapeutic match of all of them. The international crowd is low — but Montessori environments are built for exactly the kind of child who responds to adults who wait and observe. Worth a direct call before ruling it out.

Loop 2 critiqueWhere the scores break down
What we overcounted
Hägerneholm Förskola's 5 on speech therapy might be generous. 151 children, 30.6% certified teachers, no bilingual track. A large Swedish-only kommunal environment has no structural reason to run the small-group, vocabulary-focused sessions that actually work for multilingual kids with speech support needs. The convenience score is real. The fit score for Ivaan specifically is not.
What we undercounted
Olympicaförskolan might deserve an 8 on speech therapy fit. 34 children. Montessori pace. Adults who wait for the child to initiate. That's structurally what a specialpedagog would recommend for a child who responds to calm and patience. The international vibe is low — but the environment is better calibrated to how Ivaan works than any of the larger options.
What public data can't tell you
The avdelning matters more than the school. A good teacher in Hägerneholm will beat a bad one in Futuraskolan, every time. What's the actual group size in the room Ivaan would be in? Is there a specialpedagog on this site, or shared across three units? Do the staff know what to do with a child who code-switches between four languages? These questions don't exist in any database. They get answered at pickup time.

For the conversationWhat to say to Aniket

Three things. Keep it short. Send him the link, then have the conversation.

Point 1 · The proximity advantage
130 metres is not a soft benefit
Täby uses proximity as the tiebreaker when too many kids apply to a school. At 130m from Hägerneholmsskolan, Ivaan will almost certainly get priority over every other applicant for that school. That's a structural advantage that never expires, and if we have another child, syskonförtur (sibling priority, introduced 2026) means they'd likely get in too. We can still choose a different school — but we hold the strongest card for this one.
Point 2 · The honest school verdict
The local school is fine. Not the best in Täby.
Hägerneholmsskolan is above national average on results and teacher quality. SALSA +1.0 means it's adding genuine value for its students. The real concern is class sizes — 16.4 per teacher versus the national average of 12.1. For Ivaan specifically, who does better in smaller calmer environments with patient adults, that number matters. The school is not a reason to not buy 11B. It's a reason to have a backup plan, which is Futuraskolan or IES.
Point 3 · The one action that can't wait
IES queue. Today. It's free.
IES Täby is bilingual English+Swedish, structured, calm, and the school most expat families in north Stockholm want for their kids. It only starts at Year 4 so it's years away for Ivaan. But the queue is long and grows every month. Registering costs nothing and creates a future option. Not registering means losing queue position we can never recover. This is a 5-minute decision.
